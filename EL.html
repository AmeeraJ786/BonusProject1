Edgar's Lambent

A game based off the 5 stages of grief

Lambent is a play on the word lament, as light is an important ascept of the game. Edgar(the main character) deals with grief, lamenting over the death of his lover as he fights through a haunted mansion.

Inspired by Cult of the Lamb, Don't Starve, Disinherited, Moonlighter and Crypt Custodian.

(This game isn't out yet, it's just for my game design class)
